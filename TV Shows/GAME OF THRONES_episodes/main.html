Game of Thrones is set on the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, where noble families battle for control of the Iron Throne and power over the Seven Kingdoms. As alliances shift and betrayals unfold, dangerous forces rise from the North, threatening all of humanity. The story features political intrigue, epic battles, dragons, and supernatural threats in a sprawling world inspired by the “A Song of Ice and Fire” novels.
