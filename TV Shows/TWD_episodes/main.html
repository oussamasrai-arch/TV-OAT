STORY

The Walking Dead is set in a post-apocalyptic world where a deadly outbreak has caused the dead to rise and roam the earth as walkers. The story begins with former sheriff Rick Grimes, who awakens from a coma to find civilization completely collapsed. He joins a group of survivors and gradually becomes their leader as they search for food, shelter, and safety.
