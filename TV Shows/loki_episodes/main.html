STORY
LOKI follows LOKI, the Asgardian God of Mischief, after he escapes with the Tesseract during the events of Avengers: Endgame. Arrested by the mysterious Time Variance Authority (TVA) for creating a branched timeline, Loki must work with TVA agents to fix temporal disruptions while confronting alternate versions of himself and threats to the timeline. Across two seasons, Loki navigates shifting realities and the consequences of his choices as the multiverse fractures and new dangers emerge.
