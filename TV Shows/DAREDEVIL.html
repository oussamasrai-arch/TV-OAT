STORY
Daredevil follows Matt Murdock, a blind lawyer whose remaining senses are heightened to superhuman levels after a childhood accident. By day, he fights for justice in the courtroom; by night, he becomes the masked vigilante Daredevil, protecting Hell’s Kitchen from crime and corruption. As he confronts powerful enemies like Wilson Fisk, Matt struggles with morality, faith, and the cost of violence, balancing his desire for justice with the darkness within himself.
