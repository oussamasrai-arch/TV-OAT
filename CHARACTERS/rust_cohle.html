STORY
Rustin “Rust” Cohle is a brilliant but troubled detective in True Detective whose obsessive attention to detail fuels a long-running investigation. Guided by a deeply philosophical and often cynical worldview, he follows leads others overlook. His relentless pursuit of truth tests his sanity, relationships, and ability to endure what he discovers.
