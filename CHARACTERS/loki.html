STORY
Loki Laufeyson, commonly known as the God of Mischief, is a complex character from Marvel mythology who blends charm, intelligence, and deception. Raised as an Asgardian prince, he struggles with jealousy, identity, and his desire for power, often putting him at odds with his brother Thor. Despite his questionable actions, Loki shows surprising depth and growth, oscillating between villainy and reluctant heroism with his signature wit.
