Lucifer Morningstar is the lead of Lucifer, leaving Hell to run a nightclub in Los Angeles and seeking freedom on his own terms. With charm and wit, he becomes a police consultant and begins confronting questions of guilt and redemption. His journey mixes crime-solving with supernatural drama as he learns what it means to change.
