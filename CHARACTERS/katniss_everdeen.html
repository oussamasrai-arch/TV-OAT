STORY
Katniss Everdeen is the protagonist of The Hunger Games who volunteers to save her sister and is thrown into a deadly televised contest. Resourceful and fiercely protective, she becomes a reluctant symbol of rebellion against oppression. Her story blends survival, sacrifice, and the heavy cost of becoming the face of a revolution.
