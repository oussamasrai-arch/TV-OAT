STORY
Dean Winchester is one of the main protagonists of Supernatural, raised into a life of hunting monsters alongside his brother Sam. Tough, loyal, and deeply protective, Dean faces endless supernatural threats while carrying emotional burdens from his childhood and family. His mix of bravery, humor, and sacrifice defines him as a hero who fights not just for survival, but for the people he loves.
