STORY
Sameen Shaw is a former government assassin from Person of Interest known for her precise combat skills and cool‑headed approach to danger. Trained to function with minimal emotional response, she often hides her feelings behind sarcasm and blunt honesty. Despite her detached demeanor, Shaw proves fiercely loyal and dependable, forming strong bonds with her team as she fights to save others.
