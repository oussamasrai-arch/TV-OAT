STORY
Raymond ‘Red’ Reddington is a master criminal and strategist from The Blacklist, famous for his intelligence, charm, and extensive underworld connections. After years as a fugitive, he mysteriously surrenders to the FBI and offers to help them track down dangerous criminals—but only through Agent Elizabeth Keen. His motives remain layered and secretive, blending loyalty, manipulation, and emotion into a uniquely compelling persona.
