STORY
Sam Winchester is one of the main heroes of Supernatural, drawn back into hunting when danger returns to his family’s life. Intelligent and compassionate, Sam often questions destiny while fighting demons, monsters, and cosmic threats. Across his journey, he balances hope and sacrifice, constantly trying to choose the right path even when the world turns dark.
