Daenerys Targaryen is a key figure in A Song of Ice and Fire, rising from exile to become a determined claimant with a powerful sense of destiny. As she gathers allies and builds strength, she learns leadership through hardship and loss. Known as the Mother of Dragons, her journey blends conquest, liberation, and the difficult choices that come with power.
