STORY
Tyrion Lannister is a sharp‑witted nobleman from Game of Thrones, known for his intelligence, humor, and ability to navigate political danger. Often underestimated because of his stature, he relies on strategy, reading people, and diplomacy to survive in a ruthless world. Though shaped by pain and prejudice, Tyrion remains thoughtful and compassionate, making him one of the realm’s most influential figures.
