Billy Butcher is the ruthless leader of The Boys, determined to expose and stop corrupt superheroes who abuse their power. Driven by anger and a personal mission, he uses brutal tactics and relentless pressure to get results. Behind the aggression, Butcher’s story reveals loyalty, obsession, and the thin line between justice and revenge.
