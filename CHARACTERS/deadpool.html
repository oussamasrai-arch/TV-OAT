STORY
Deadpool, whose real name is Wade Winston Wilson, is a fictional anti‑hero appearing in Marvel Comics. Known for his regenerative healing ability, exceptional combat skills, and dark humor, he frequently breaks the fourth wall to speak directly to the audience. Originally a mercenary, he became infamous as ‘The Merc with a Mouth’ for his nonstop chatter and unpredictable behavior, blending action, comedy, and satire throughout his stories.
