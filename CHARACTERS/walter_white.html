STORY
Walter White is a chemistry teacher in Breaking Bad whose life changes after a devastating diagnosis pushes him into the criminal world. Using his intelligence, he becomes a meth manufacturer and gradually transforms into the feared Heisenberg. His story explores pride, power, and how one decision can spiral into irreversible darkness.
