STORY
Patrick Jane is the clever consultant in The Mentalist, famous for extraordinary observation skills and psychological tricks. After a tragic past reshapes his life, he uses charm and misdirection to solve cases while pursuing personal justice. His playful intelligence hides deeper grief, making his victories feel both strategic and emotional.
