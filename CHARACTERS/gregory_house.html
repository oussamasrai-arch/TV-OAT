STORY
Dr. Gregory House is a genius diagnostician in House whose unmatched logic helps solve rare medical mysteries. Known for sarcasm, blunt honesty, and breaking rules, he clashes with coworkers while pursuing the truth behind each case. Beneath the arrogance, House carries pain and addiction, making him as complicated as the patients he saves.
