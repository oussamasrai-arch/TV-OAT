STORY
John Reese is a former CIA operative in Person of Interest who teams up with Harold Finch to prevent violent crimes predicted by an advanced surveillance system. Calm and highly skilled, he operates in the shadows to protect people who don’t know they’re in danger. His journey is defined by loyalty, redemption, and the question of how far one should go to save others.
