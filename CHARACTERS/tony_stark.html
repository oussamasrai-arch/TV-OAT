STORY
Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, is a billionaire inventor and the genius behind some of the most advanced technology in the Marvel universe. After being gravely injured and held captive, he built his first armored suit to escape, marking the beginning of his transformation from weapons manufacturer to hero. Driven by intelligence, charisma, and a complicated past, Stark balances humor with responsibility as he protects the world using his evolving Iron Man suits.
